First five years

In the first five years of my life, my days were filled with a peaceful simplicity. world revolved around eating, sleeping, and exploring the wonders of my surroundings with a curious yet innocent mind. My heart was light, and my mind, free of worries, found joy in the smallest of things. There was one particular incident from my childhood that I remember vividly. It happened one sunny afternoon when I was playing outside, full of energy and enthusiasm. I was running around, my laughter echoing in the air, when I tripped over a small rock. I fell forward and hit my forehead, resulting in a small injury. I cried out in pain, but soon enough, my family rushed to my side, comforting me with their warm embraces and soothing words. Despite the injury, I continued to be a happy and carefree child. My days were filled with play, laughter, and the love of my family. However, I always had a yearning to learn more, to understand the world beyond my immediate experiences. I longed for a place where I could begin my journey of education. One day, my family decided it was time for me to start attending nursery school. I was excited at the prospect of learning new things and making new friends. The first day of nursery school was a big step for me, a chance to expand my horizons and start my educational journey.
